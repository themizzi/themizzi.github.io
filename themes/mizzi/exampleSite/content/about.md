This is an example about page for the Mizzi Hugo theme. Customize this content to tell your story.

The Mizzi theme is perfect for:

* Musicians who want to showcase their work
* Developers and technologists
* Anyone who wants a clean, modern website
* People who need both light and dark mode support

The theme includes special content types for music-related content like artists, albums, tracks, and events, making it easy to build a comprehensive music website.
